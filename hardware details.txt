## 1. Sensor Configuration (per sensor, repeat 8×)
**IR LED driver:** two 220 Ω 0805 resistors in parallel → **110 Ω**  
**Phototransistor load:** **10 kΩ** (keeps noise low, still gives a clean 1.5 V–2 V black/white swing)

**Schematic of one sensor module**
```
+5V ──┬──[ 220Ω (0805) ]──┐
      │                    ├── IR LED anode (→ cathode → GND)
      └──[ 220Ω (0805) ]──┘

+5V ──[ 10 kΩ ]──┬── PIC analog input (AN0–AN7)
                  │
          Phototransistor collector
                  │
          Phototransistor emitter ── GND
```
- IR LED current ≈ 34.5 mA (safe for standard 5 mm 940 nm LEDs)
- 8 sensors spaced **9 mm centre‑to‑centre** as required
- All 8 analog outputs go directly to the microcontroller’s AN0–AN7

## 3. Button Circuit (Active High on RB0)

```

                             │
                            [10 kΩ]   (pull‑down)
                             │
                            GND
```

- 10 kΩ pull‑down ensures solid low when released

## 5. Motor Drivers (2× DRV8871)

| Driver     | Signal | PIC Pin | Function                       |
|------------|--------|---------|--------------------------------|
| Left Motor | IN1    | **RC2** | Speed – CCP1 PWM               |
|            | IN2    | **RC0** | Direction (digital)            |
| Right Motor| IN1    | **RC1** | Speed – CCP2 PWM               |
|            | IN2    | **RC4** | Direction (digital)            |

**Logic table for each DRV8871:**
- **Forward:** IN1 = PWM, IN2 = 0
- **Reverse:** IN1 = 0, IN2 = PWM
- **Coast:**  IN1 = 0, IN2 = 0
- **Brake:**  IN1 = 1, IN2 = 1

No extra interface needed – direct connection from PIC to DRV8871 inputs.

## 6. ICSP (Programming Header)

Standard 5‑pin connector (PICkit‑style):
```
Pin 1 → MCLR (PIC pin 1)     [with 10kΩ to +5V]
Pin 2 → VDD (+5V)
Pin 3 → GND
Pin 4 → PGD (RB7, pin 40)
Pin 5 → PGC (RB6, pin 39)
```
- Keep RB3/PGM floating (no connection).

## 7. Power Supply & Decoupling

- **Regulator:** LM7805 (or a 5 V buck converter) supplied by a 7.4 V Li‑Po or 6×AA battery pack.
- **Capacitors near regulator:**
  - Input: 10 µF electrolytic
  - Output: 10 µF + 100 nF ceramic
- **At PIC VDD pins (11 & 32):** 100 nF ceramic to GND, placed as close as possible.
- **Optional bulk:** 100 µF electrolytic on the 5 V rail.
